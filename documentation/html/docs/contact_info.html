AFSIM Contact Information¶

Community¶

Visit the AFSIM Portal on DI2E (Access Controlled) for the latest information about AFSIM and its community.

Developer Resources¶

Use the AFSIM Developer Resources on DI2E (Access Controlled) to access and contribute to the AFSIM codebase managed in Git.

Support¶

AFSIM Management Team¶

To contact government members of the AFSIM product management team, please email afrl.rq.afsim@us.af.mil. This email address supports encryption.

AFSIM Help Desk¶

Visit the AFSIM Helpdesk on DI2E (Access Controlled) to report a problem or get support from a community of expert users and developers.

Issues and Bug Reports¶

Visit the AFSIM Helpdesk on DI2E (Access Controlled) to browse known issues or submit a bug report.

Enhancements¶

Use the AFSIM Community Resources on DI2E (Access Controlled) to suggest a change, propose a new feature, or generally contribute to the discussion on the future of AFSIM.
